Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At Curaçao Weather ATC (curacaoweatheratc.uk), we respect your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected when you visit the site and how it's used.

Information We Collect

We may collect non‑personal information such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • General location (country/region)

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us by email.

Cookies & Consent

This is a free personal project. On your first visit you'll see a cookie banner asking whether to allow analytics cookies. Nothing beyond strictly necessary site functionality runs until you accept — the site uses Google's Consent Mode v2, so Google Analytics collects no cookie‑based data until you opt in. You can change your choice at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain. There is no advertising on this site.

Third‑Party Services

Once consent is granted, the following third‑party services may set cookies or collect data:

  • Google Analytics — anonymized traffic/usage statistics, to understand how the site is used
  • Embedded weather and map providers (Windy, ADS‑B Exchange, NOAA, CheckWX, Open‑Meteo) used to display live data

You can view Google's data practices at policies.google.com.

How We Use Information

  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze traffic patterns
  • Maintain site security

Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect data; however, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Links to External Sites

Our website links to and embeds third‑party sites (weather, radar, flight and ATC data providers). We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices — please review their own policies.
